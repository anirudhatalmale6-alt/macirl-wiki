Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

What is IPTV?

IPTV (Internet Protocol Television) streams live TV channels and on-demand content over the internet. No satellite dish or antenna needed - just an internet connection and a compatible device.

What devices are supported?

Android phones/tablets, Amazon Firestick, iOS devices, Smart TVs (Samsung, LG, Sony), Windows, Mac, MAG boxes, Formuler, Enigma2/Zgemma, and more. Check the setup guides for your specific device.

How fast do I get my login?

Most orders are set up within 1-2 hours. During busy times it may take up to 12 hours.

What internet speed do I need?

Minimum 25 Mbps for HD content, 50 Mbps+ recommended for 4K. A wired ethernet connection will always give the best performance.

Do you offer trials?

Yes! Open a ticket in our Discord to request a 24-hour trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

PayPal, bank transfer, and crypto. Payment details are provided when you open an order ticket.

Can I use it on multiple devices?

Each subscription includes 1 connection by default. Multi-connection packages are available at a discounted rate - ask when ordering.

What if the service goes down?

Open a support ticket in Discord and we'll investigate immediately. We monitor our servers 24/7.

Do I need a VPN?

Not required, but recommended. Some ISPs may throttle streaming traffic. A VPN prevents this and adds privacy. Check our VPN Guide for recommendations.

IPTV Setup - Android

Setup guide for Android phones, tablets, TV boxes, and Android TV.

Android PhoneAndroid TabletAndroid TV BoxAndroid TV

Option 1: TiviMate (Recommended)

Best for: Live TV
TiviMate is the most popular and feature-rich IPTV player for Android. Free version available, premium unlocks recording and multiple playlists.
1
Download TiviMate
Open the Google Play Store and search for "TiviMate IPTV Player". Install it.
2
Add Playlist
Open TiviMate and tap "Add Playlist". Select "Xtream Codes" as the playlist type.
3
Enter Your Credentials
Enter your server URL, username, and password (these are provided after purchase in your Discord ticket).
4
Load Channels
Tap "Next" and wait for all channels and EPG (TV guide) data to load. This may take a minute.
5
Start Watching
Browse categories, use the EPG guide, or search for specific channels. Enjoy!

Option 2: IPTV Smarters Pro

1
Download IPTV Smarters Pro
Get it from the Google Play Store - search "IPTV Smarters Pro".
2
Login
Select "Login with Xtream Codes API". Enter any name, your server URL, username, and password.
3
Choose Content
Select Live TV, Movies, or Series from the main menu. Browse and enjoy!

Option 3: OTT Navigator

1
Download OTT Navigator
Search "OTT Navigator IPTV" in the Play Store and install.
2
Add Provider
Go to Settings > Providers > Add new. Select "Xtream Codes" and enter your credentials.
3
Done
Channels will load automatically. Use the channel list or EPG to browse.

IPTV Setup - Amazon Firestick

Setup guide for Firestick, Fire TV, and Fire TV Cube.

FirestickFire TVFire TV Cube

Step 1: Enable Unknown Sources

1
Open Settings
From the Firestick home screen, go to Settings > My Fire TV > Developer Options.
2
Enable Apps from Unknown Sources
Turn ON "Apps from Unknown Sources" (or "Install Unknown Apps"). If you see a list, enable it for "Silk Browser" or "Downloader".

Step 2: Install Downloader App

3
Get Downloader
Go to the Amazon App Store (search icon on home screen). Search for "Downloader" by AFTVnews. Install it.

Step 3: Install TiviMate

4
Download TiviMate
Open Downloader. Enter the download URL for TiviMate APK (ask in your Discord support ticket for the link). Download and install.
5
Setup TiviMate
Open TiviMate. Select "Add Playlist" > "Xtream Codes". Enter your server URL, username, and password.
6
Enjoy
Wait for channels to load. Use the remote to browse channels, access the EPG guide, and watch content.
Pro Tip
For the best experience on Firestick, use an ethernet adapter for a wired connection. This eliminates buffering caused by weak WiFi signals.

IPTV Setup - Smart TV

Setup guide for Samsung, LG, and other smart TVs.

SamsungLGSonyOther

Samsung Smart TV

1
Open Samsung App Store
Press the Smart Hub / Apps button on your remote. Search for "IPTV Smarters" or "Smart IPTV".
2
Install and Open
Install the app and open it. For IPTV Smarters, enter your Xtream Codes credentials.

If using Smart IPTV: note the MAC address shown on screen. Go to siptv.app/mylist on your phone. Enter the MAC address and your M3U URL. Click "Send". Restart the app.

LG Smart TV

1
Open LG Content Store
Press the Home button and open the LG Content Store. Search for "Smart IPTV".

Note MAC Address
Install and open the app. Note the MAC address displayed on screen.
3
Upload Playlist
On your phone/computer, go to siptv.app/mylist. Enter your TV's MAC address and your M3U URL. Click "Send". Restart the app on your TV.
Older TVs
If your Smart TV doesn't have an app store or the apps above, consider connecting a Firestick or Android TV box to your TV instead. Check the Firestick or Android setup guides.

IPTV Setup - MAG Box

Setup guide for MAG 254, 256, 322, 324, 420, and other MAG devices.

1
Get Your MAC Address
Go to Settings > System Info on your MAG box. Note the MAC address (starts with 00:1A:79). Send this to us in your Discord ticket.
2
Set Portal URL
Go to Settings > Servers > Portals. Set Portal 1 Name to "MACIRL". Set Portal 1 URL to the portal URL we provide.
3
Save and Restart
Save the settings and restart the MAG box. The portal will load automatically with all channels.
Important
MAG boxes use MAC-based authentication. You must provide your MAC address before we can activate your service.

Troubleshooting

Fix common issues quickly.

IPTV Issues

Buffering / Freezing

This is almost always an internet or ISP issue. Try the following:

1
Check your internet speed at speedtest.net - you need at least 25 Mbps
2
Switch from WiFi to a wired ethernet connection if possible
3
Try using a VPN - your ISP may be throttling streaming traffic
4
Clear the app cache and restart
5
Try a different IPTV player app

Cannot Login / Connection Failed

1
Double-check your username and password - they are case sensitive
2
Make sure the server URL is correct (no extra spaces)
3
Check if your subscription has expired
4
Try a different app to rule out app-specific issues

No EPG (TV Guide)

1
Go to the app settings and manually refresh/update the EPG
2
Wait up to 24 hours for EPG data to fully populate
3
Make sure your timezone is set correctly in the app settings

Media Server Issues

Library Not Showing

1
Make sure you accepted the email invite
2
Sign out and back in on the app
3
Check for app updates

Playback Issues

1
Lower the streaming quality in the app settings
2
Use a wired connection for 4K content
3
Try "Direct Play" instead of transcoding in the app settings
Still stuck?
Open a support ticket in our Discord server. Include your device type, the app you're using, and screenshots of any error messages.

VPN Guide

Why you should use a VPN and which ones we recommend.

Why Use a VPN?

A VPN (Virtual Private Network) encrypts your internet traffic and prevents your ISP from seeing what you're streaming. Benefits include:

1
Prevent ISP Throttling
Some ISPs deliberately slow down streaming traffic. A VPN hides your activity so they can't throttle you.
2
Privacy
Your ISP can't log what you watch or stream. Your activity stays private.
3
Security
Encrypts your connection on public WiFi networks.
